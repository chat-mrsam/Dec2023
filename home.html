Merry Christmas
This year, you have to solve a puzzle to get your gift.

Solve the following riddle.

I am a phrase, a break from cold's embrace,
In a locale where warmth takes winter's place.
Beside a place, where crashes cause play,
On shifty foundations, one might say.
With a group, rarely seen, both far and close
What am I, can you discern this plot's details?
